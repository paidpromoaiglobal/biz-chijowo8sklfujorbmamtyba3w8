About Us

SMART HINDI ACADEMY

Smart Hindi Academy is a premier coaching centre located in Sri Sai Nagar, Thoraipakkam, Chennai, dedicated to providing high-quality Hindi language education to students of all ages. Led by the highly acclaimed and passionate educator Mrs. Kalpana, the academy offers a warm, nurturing learning environment where every student receives personalized attention and concept-based instruction. With a perfect 5-star rating from 36 satisfied parents and students, Smart Hindi Academy has earned a stellar reputation across Chennai's OMR corridor.

At Smart Hindi Academy, we believe that language learning goes beyond textbooks – it's about building confidence, clarity, and a lifelong love for Hindi. Our curriculum covers Hindi language coaching, Mathematics, and preparation for competitive exams like the Parichay examination. With multiple convenient batch timings across morning, afternoon, and evening slots six days a week, we make quality education accessible and flexible for every family. Join us and experience the difference that dedicated, expert teaching can make in your child's academic journey.

  • Perfect 5-star rating with 36 glowing reviews from happy parents and students
